COURSES OFFERED

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

Prepares students to design, develop, and manage computer-based information systems, with a focus on practical IT solutions for businesses and organizations.

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

Equips students with the skills and knowledge to excel in the hospitality and tourism industry, including hotel operations, event planning, and customer service.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)

Focuses on computing theories, programming, algorithms, and software development, preparing students for careers in software engineering and tech innovation.

Bachelor of Science in Multimedia Arts (BMMA)

Combines art, design, and technology to train students in digital media, animation, graphic design, and interactive content creation.

Bachelor of Secondary Education Major in English (BSEd)

Prepares future educators to teach English at the secondary level, with training in language, literature, communication, and teaching strategies.
